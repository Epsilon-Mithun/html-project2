Solensia is the once-monthly, vet-administered monoclonal antibody therapy for the control of pain of feline osteoarthritis(OA)

  • 77% of cost owners reported improvement in signs of pain after their cats were treated with Solensia for 3 months in a study.
  • Controls the pain of OA in cats, helping to improve their mobility, comfort and well-being.
  • Solensia was found to be well tolerated in filed studies that included cats identified as IRIS stage 1 to 2.

Based on total improvement of pain scores after three monthly doses using a pet owner assesment Total N = 131 of 267 treated cats were identified as IRIS stage 1 to 2.
